Tacos

Ingredients

  • 1 chorizo sausage link
  • 2 tablespoons chipotle peppers in adobo sauce
  • 4 corn tortillas
  • 2 tablespoons chopped onoion, or to taste
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro, or to taste

Steps

  1. Combine crumbled chorizo and chipotle peppers in adobo sauce in a bowl.
  2. Heat a skillet over medium-high heat and add chorizo mixture and cook until crisp.
  3. Heat tortillas in the same skillet over medium heat until warmed.
  4. Stack 2 tortillas for each taco, then fill with chorizo, onion and cilantro.
  5. Enjoy!
