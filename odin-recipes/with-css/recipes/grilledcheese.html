Grilled cheese and tomato soup

Ingredients

Grilled cheese

  • 4 slices white bread
  • 3 tablespoons butter, divided
  • 2 slices Cheddar cheese

Tomato soup

  • 4 cups chopped fresh tomatoes
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 1 large slice of onion
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons white sugar, or to taste
  • 1 teaspoon salt, or to taste

Steps

Grilled cheese

  1. Heat a nonstick skillet over medium heat. Butter one side of a slice of bread and place the side on the hot skillet. Add 1 slice of cheese and butter a second slice of bread on one side and place butter-side up on top of cheese.
  2. Cook until lightly browned on one side, flip over and cook until cheese is melted.
  3. Repeat for the remaining 2 slices of bread.
  4. Enjoy with tomato soup!

Tomato soup

  1. Combine tomatoes, chicken broth, garlic cloves, and a large slice of onion in a stockpot over medium heat. Bring to a boil and let simmer for 20 minutes.
  2. Remove from heat and run the mixture through a food mill, discard any stuff left over in the food mill.
  3. Melt butter over medium heat in the now empty pot. Stir in flour, whisk constantly until mixture turns medium brown.
  4. Gradually whisk in a bit of the tomato mixture
  5. Season with sugar and salt to taste
  6. Serve hot with grilled cheese and enjoy!
